About Me

I am a passionate and detail oriented Computer Science student (Software Engineering major) with a strong interest in web development. My journey into technology began with curiosity about how websites and applications work behind the scenes, and that curiosity quickly developed into a passion for building responsive, user friendly web sites. I enjoy working with HTML5 and CSS3 to create clean, structured, and accessible designs. I am particularly interested in responsive design principles and modern layout techniques such as Flexbox which allow me to build websites that function seamlessly across different device types. Beyond technical skills, I am a problem solver who enjoys breaking down complex challenges into manageable solutions. I believe that good design is not only about appearance but also usability and accessibility. My goal is to grow into a professional full-stack developer, continuously improving my skills while contributing to innovative and impactful digital projects.
